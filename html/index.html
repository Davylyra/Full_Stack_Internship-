An approachable, elevated dining experience

Davies Restaurant blends seasonal creativity with approachable flavors. We partner with nearby farms and fisheries to build a menu that changes with the harvest. Whether you're celebrating a special evening or stopping by for a relaxed night out, our team ensures a memorable meal with thoughtful service and a welcoming atmosphere.

Chef's Highlights

  • Signature Jollof Rice

    Fragrant long-grain rice simmered in spiced tomato blend, served with caramelized plantain and your choice of grilled chicken or fish — smoky, vibrant, and fully Ghanaian.

  • Banku & Grilled Tilapia

    Traditional fermented corn and cassava dumpling, paired with whole grilled tilapia and house shito — hearty, tangy, and bold.

  • Kelewele

    Sweet ripe plantain cubes tossed in ginger, chili and warm spices, fried until crisp — a favorite snack and dessert.

From our Chef
